About this game

Gris is a hopeful young woman, lost in her own world, dealing with a painful experience. Her journey through grief manifests itself in her dress, which grants her new abilities to better navigate her faded reality. Throughout the story, Gris evolves emotionally and begins to see the world in a different way, revealing new paths to explore using his new abilities.

GRIS is a serene and evocative experience, with no dangers, frustrations or deaths. The player must explore a meticulously crafted world, with a delicate style, detailed animations and an elegant soundtrack. Throughout the game, simple puzzles, platforming sequences and optional skill-based challenges will reveal themselves as you open up the world of Gris.

GRIS is an almost textless experience, with just simple control reminders represented by universal icons. Anyone can play, whatever their language.
